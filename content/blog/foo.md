Memory safety is often discussed as a technical requirement or a security primitive. In the context of Rust, it's frequently the lead story. But when we look at software through the lens of a consultant, memory safety becomes something much more profound: it is a social contract.

## Trust as a System Property

A system that cannot crash due to a null pointer is a system that allows developers to sleep. More importantly, it allows teams to move faster with confidence. In this post, we explore the organizational impact of choosing memory-safe languages for mission-critical infrastructure.
